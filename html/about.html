About Us

About Creative Nook

  • Creative Nook is family owned and operated Early Childhood Service. We cater for age 6 weeks to 5 years old.

  • Creative Nook aims to provide a warm, caring, and safe environment where all children can thrive, feel safe, secure, and supported.

  • Creative Nook believe that a holistic approach with a strong focus on imagination, creativity and freedom of choice will support children to develop their self-esteem, passion for learning and understanding of the world they live in.

  • We believe that children have rights, and it plays a fundamental role in how we interact, educate and care for children.

  • We aim to celebrate and embrace the uniqueness and diversity of all children, helping then find their voice and discover their sense of being, belonging and becoming in our community and wider world.

What We Provide

We provide everything for your child’s needs. High quality unique educational program, Educational STEM progrm, Incursions and excursions, 5 times nutritional meals, milk.

Nappies and nappy cream, linen for all children Hats and Sunscreen Extra curricular activities to enhance children’s physical and cognitive development. Daily communication with parents through Story Park App.
Also,


  • Meals

    Creative Nook has partnered with KGF (Kids Gourmet Food). They have been providing fresh, nutritious, homestyle meals for children in childcare since 2002. They believe that providing a variety of nutritious meals in early years builds positive eating habits in the long term. The 6 weeks menu provides a variety of cultural food for children to taste and explore. The meals are prepared keeping Australin Health Guidelines in mind.
