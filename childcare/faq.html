FAQ's (Frequently Asked Questions)

Starting at Creative Nook

How to book a tour?
You can complete our online form or simply call us on 02 9618 2252
What are your operating hours?
We are open 50 weeks per year. We are open Monday to Friday 7am to 6pm. We are closed on public holidays.
What makes us different?
We provide everything for your child’s needs. Our service is unique. We understand that children are unique individuals and they learn through their head, heart and hands. We will support their journey in the big world by providing pathways to all necessary skills. We will cater for their individual needs to ensure that they thrive in our care. Our high quality, interest based education focuses on imagination, creativity and experimental education to allow children to build their social, emotional and physical wellbeing which will then lead to quality language and cognitive development.
How to enroll?
Visit us at 93 Saywell Rd, Macquarie Field and have a look at our amazing service. Talk to our service director and amazing educators. We will provide you will all necessary information for your child to start. Simply, (Enroll Here ) or contact us on 02 9618 2252
DayCare Fees/Charges?
Our fees are set per day and you pay gap fees after calculating CCS. Use our CCS calculator here, CCS Calculator to help you estimate your fees accordingly.

Services and Facilities

What is included in fees?
Please check what we provide on about us page here
Do you provide Food?
Yes, we provide 5 times meals. Our menus are designed to suit all children and their dietary requirements. Our menu is halal, nut free and egg free. We provide breakfast, Morning tea, afternoon tea, lunch and late snacks. The children have access to fruits throughout the day. We provide milk with their morning and afternoon tea as well.
What is your policy regarding visiting children during their care?
Yes, our open door policy will allow you to come and visit the children at any time of the day. Our stay and play roster for parents also allows parents to book a time to come join us in our sessions and take part in our activities with the children.
If my baby needs breast milk, what facility do you have?
We encourage breast feeding for children. We have a breast-feeding facility at our service. You are welcome to attend the service to breast feed while your child is in our care. Alternatively, you can also express and provide us in a bottle and we will provide it to your child according to their needs.
How do you manage complaints?
We understand the importance of care and wellbeing of all children. Our priority is the health and safety of all children, educators, and visitors.

We recognize that all concerns are to be handled promptly and appropriately. If you voice your concern, we will take it seriously and we will respond to it promptly.

We will always maintain privacy and confidentiality.
We will discuss feedback and solution of the issue personally with you to ensure that you are happy with our service and action that we take.

The Staff at Creative Nook

What is your ratio?
We provide high quality ratio at all times and follow the NSW regulatory requirement for ratios as per the following:
  • 0-2 years – 1:4 (1 educator to 4 children)
  • 2-3 years- 1:5
  • 3-5 years- 1:10
Additionally, we also have additional support educators, who provide care to children throughout the day to enhance our Inclusive Practice and support our educators, especially during the busy time of the day.
What Qualification do your staff have?
All our educators are highly qualified. Diploma qualified educators lead our rooms. Certificate 3 qualified educators assist in the room. Our team is led by an Early childhood Teacher, who has over 16 years working experience in the childcare industry and has worked as a leader for over 10 years. All educators are CPR, first aid, Asthma anaphylaxis qualified. They all have current working with children’s check
How do educators communicate with families?
Our educators communicate with families via the STORYPARK App. The routine and children’s day will be published online for families to see how their children are doing throughout the day. The educators also communicate with families at their pick up and drop off time.

Education at Creative Nook

What is your educational program like?
Our Curriculum is created using the early Years Learning Framework and Steiner’s theory that children learn through their heart, head and hand. Our curriculum provides holistic approach to learning. We also provide unique school readiness program for children to transition to primary school. Click here, to find out more.
Do you provide any extracurricular activities?
Yes. We provide soccer programs, dance and Movements and Physical wellbeing programs for all children. Additionally, we also organise educational incursions and excursions for children throughout the year.
Is your center Inclusive
Our service is inclusive. We promote children’s culture and diversity and celebrate them as a part of our curriculum. The Service also offers support for children with additional needs. Our staff are trained to care for children with additional needs. We also work with external inclusion agency and professionals to support children and make them feel included in everything that we do.
