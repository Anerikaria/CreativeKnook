Covid19 Quetions
What is the centre required to do in positive cases?
As per NSW government regulation, your child's centre will not be able to notify you every time a child or staff member takes the COVID test; however, you will be informed if the results raise any concerns (regulation 88(2)) provides for this provision. OR Parents and caregivers will be notified if a child has been exposed to a positive case and given information about testing and isolation recommendations by NSW Health.
What is the family required to do in case of positive cases?
A family should consider the possibility of transmitting COVID-19 to other family members, especially those at risk of severe illness, when considering whether to send their child to the centre during this time. NOTE: for more information on COVID-19, please visit www.education.gov.au/covid-19/childcare
