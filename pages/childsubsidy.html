How CCS Works?

Note : - Our Service is CCS approved. There are 2 main components that are tested while applying for subsidy.

Activity Hours
Hours of care, based on the activity of the least active parent.

Activity level is decided fortnightly. for eg. If Less than 8 hours activity level each fortnight than hours of subsidised care each fortnight is 0 if you earn above $72,466 and 24 hours if you earn $72,466 or below.
and If more than 8 to 16 activity level each fortnight than hours of subsided care each fornight would be 36 hours. and so on..

For more information on how activity hours work, to check hours of care based on that and what is recognised activities includes please check here
CCS Percentage
CCS percentage, based on total annual income of the family.
CCS Calculation On Weekly Basis
This is weekly calculator for one child. CCS Percentage works based on total activity hours of fortnightly and family texable income. Based on total days of week and age of your child we calculate the weekly charge and calculate the total CCS available.
