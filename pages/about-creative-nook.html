About Us

About Creative Nook

    Creative Nook is family owned and operated Early Childhood Service that provides care and education to children age 6 weeks to 5 years old.


    Creative Nook aims to provide a warm, caring, and safe environment where all children can thrive, feel safe, secure, and supported.


    We believe that a holistic approach with a strong focus on imagination, creativity and freedom of choice will support children to develop their self-esteem, passion for learning and understanding of the world they live in.


    We believe that children have rights, and it plays a fundamental role in how we interact, educate and care for children.


    We aim to celebrate and embrace the uniqueness and diversity of all children, helping them find their voice and discover their sense of being, belonging and becoming in our community and wider world.

What We Provide

We provide everything for your child’s needs, including:



  • High quality unique educational program
  • Educational STEM program
  • Incursions and excursions
  • Nappies and nappy cream
  • Linen for all children
  • Hats and Sunscreen
  • Extra curricular activities to enhance children’s physical and cognitive development
  • Daily communication through Story Park App.

  • Meals : Creative Nook has partnered with KGF (Kids Gourmet Food). Since 2002, KGF is providing fresh, nutritional and home cooked meals for children to build positive eating habits. They believe that early exposure to a variety of nutritious meals is essential for the development of long-term healthy eating habits. The 6 weeks menu provides a variety of cultural food for children to taste and explore. The meals are prepared keeping Australin Health Guidelines in mind.
