Meet Our Team

Meet Creative Nooks Team

Our educators are part of our local community. They are experienced and are passionate about caring and educating children at Creative Nook. Our educators share a vision of holistic approach and are committed in developing a long-lasting relationship with children, their families and community. Our educators are provided with ongoing training and support to ensure that we provide best care and educational support to all children.

Khushboo Vhora

I have over 16 years’ experience working in the childcare industry. I began my journey as a primary school teacher in 2008. I have been working as a leader for over 10 years now. I was always encouraged by my parents to make hard choices and take decisions that benefit everyone.I was taught to respect others value, personapties and cultures and accept the people for who they are.


This has allowed me to understand the importance of community and families in children’s lives. I believe that families and community play an important role in children’s education.


I strive to create a learning space that is positive, safe, calm, creative, sustainable and caring. I believe that families are a core foundation of any service and gaining their trust and establishing a connection with them will allow us to create the best possible educational environment for all the children in our care.


I also strive to become a positive role model by mentoring staff in all possible ways to make sure that they enjoy working at Creative Nook Early Learning Centre.
